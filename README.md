# 🧮 The Mathetia (数学之域)

> *"Algebra, functions, trigonometry...and calculus... Why does it all have to be so complicated?"*

Welcome to **The Mathetia**, a unique adventure game developed in the Godot Engine where the dreaded subjects of high school and college mathematics come to life! This is a capstone project that blends educational concepts with engaging gameplay and storytelling.

## 📖 Story 故事背景

The game follows **X**, a student who is desperately cramming for his math finals late at night. Overwhelmed by textbooks, formulas, and the sheer complexity of math, his eyelids grow heavy, and he falls into a deep slumber. 

When he wakes up, he finds himself trapped in **The Mathetia**—a surreal realm governed by mathematical laws, where abstract concepts have manifested as powerful entities. To escape this dream and conquer his math anxiety, X must face and defeat the personifications of the very subjects he hates.

## 👾 Bosses & Realms 挑战首领

Prepare to face the guardians of the mathematical world:
- **Rational Hypnotist (有理催眠师)** - The master of algebra and rational expressions.
- **Function Siren (函数海妖)** - A deceptive entity wielding domains, ranges, and transformations.
- **Trigonometry Brother (三角兄弟)** - Twins of Sine and Cosine that will test your angle identities.
- **Calculus Emperor (微积分大帝)** - The ultimate ruler of limits, derivatives, and integrals.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **[Godot Engine](https://godotengine.org/) (GLES2)** - The open-source 2D/3D game engine.
- Structured with an elegant hierarchy including a gallery, seamless transitions, and dialog systems.

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Clone this repository.
2. Open the **Godot Engine** (compatible with Godot 3.x given the GLES2 setting).
3. Import the `project.godot` file.
4. Run the project (Default main scene is `HomePage.tscn`).
